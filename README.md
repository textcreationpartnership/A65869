##Content Summary##

#####Front#####

1. INTRODUCTION Our Christian Testimony reassum'd in the Affirmative.

#####Body#####

1. I. The Testimony of the Twelve Witnesses against Fr. Bugg's great Abuse, reassum'd and enlarg'd in the Negative, or Denial of his false Charge and Perversions.

    _ He proceeds to his Ridiculous Tryal and Examination as followeth, viz.

1. II. The aforesaid Testimony Vindicated against Fr. Bugg his gross Abuses and foul Perversions herein examined.

    _ Some of F. B's Abuses in Citation.

1. THE Self-Condemn'd APOSATE, OR Francis Bugg AGAINST FRANCIS BUGG. In His Apparent Contradictions.

    _  His Confession in his own Words.

    _ His Opposition, with Additional Notes (inclosed in Square Parentheses) from his Confession.

1. SOME REMARKS Upon F. B.'s former CONFESSIONS; FARTHER Arguing and Evincing his Self-condemnation and Apostacy, his Hypocrisie and Temporising.

1. POSTSCRIPT.

#####Back#####

1. ERRATA.
